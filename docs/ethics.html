Autonomous Drones and Warfare

Our team decided to discuss the use of autonomous drones for warfare as the highlight of our ethics case study. Over the past few years, nations have been investing in the production of "Remotely Piloted Aircrafts" or autonomous drones, which are aircrafts with controlled flight by a human operator at a distant base station. Advanced drones can be desgined with an artificial intelligence that will allow for the complete autonomous functionality of the drone without a human operator. There has been debate regarding the legality of using such technology in warfare as technology seems to outpace the current laws in place, and the morality of using drones since they are capable of taking human life.
